Another Sky — The Sky Knows. They Know. He Does Not.

Som lives an ordinary life in Dhaka—long workdays, a fragile mother at home, and memories of someone he once loved. But one night, the sky glitches.

The moon flickers unnaturally. Shadows twist where they shouldn’t. Reality feels… broken.

Soon, Som notices things no one else seems to see. History doesn’t match his memories. Strange signals pulse across the airwaves. Villages fall silent overnight. And a dark, oily rain begins to drip from the sky, suffocating the light.

Then the impossible happens.

Nine people closest to him — including his lover — vanish. Not missing. Not dead. Erased. No photos. No records. No memory of their existence. Except his.

As the world grows stranger and colder, Som feels the creeping certainty of something watching, waiting, calculating. And in that silence, a horrifying truth becomes clear: This may not be his world.
